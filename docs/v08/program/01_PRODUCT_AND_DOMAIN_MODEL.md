# 01 — Product and Domain Model

## Metaphor rule

Borrow operations, not appearances.

For each borrowed music concept record:

1. original material problem;
2. TAROKE problem;
3. surviving operation;
4. medium difference;
5. interaction;
6. accessible alternative.

## Canonical terms

**Work** — complete editable procedural poem.

**Source** — identity, lineage, statement, credits.

**Bank** — authored collection of selectable textual samples. Not a track or stem.

**Sample** — one selectable textual unit: word, phrase, fragment, punctuation-bearing expression, or other authored string.

**Relative weight** — share relative to competitors. Not a percentage.

**Chance** — independent 0–100% conditional test.

**Role** — noun, verb, adjective, adverb, preposition, command, literal, mixed.

**Form** — requested transformation such as plural or third-person singular.

**Keep text unchanged** — public label for bypassing transformations; compatible persisted field may remain `lockedLiteral`.

**Device** — line-producing instrument with named inputs and routes.

**Route** — weighted template variation.

**Pattern** — reusable stanza pattern.

**Scene** — Flow configuration activating a pattern.

**Flow Score** — ordered temporal arrangement of scenes.

**Trigger** — WHEN → THEN automation rule.

**Cue** — private audition that does not disturb Surface.

**Surface** — public/current generative output.

**Monitor** — aligned procedural information.

**Unmix Line** — exact construction history of one line.

**Take** — captured event plus provenance and annotation.

## Probability contract

Relative weight shows:

- editable weight;
- derived expected share;
- empirical recent activity, clearly separate.

Chance shows percent.

Rules:

- chance 0 never fires;
- 100 always passes when eligible;
- no chance call before eligibility;
- one chance call per eligible trigger.

New bulk paste may offer merging exact repeated entries into weight, but imports are never silently normalized.

## Forms

Three levels:

1. global case/compound policy;
2. bank role policy;
3. selected-sample exception.

Only relevant fields appear:

- noun → plural;
- verb → third-person singular;
- literal/adjective/adverb/preposition → no irrelevant noun/verb fields;
- mixed → explicit choice.

## Cue / Surface

Cue generates isolated local output, may use fixed seed, and never modifies Surface history.

Surface is the running whole and preserves reader inspection.

Preview is separate: sandboxed exported artifact.

## Medium difference

Do not translate:

- volume into meaning;
- waveform into unreadable abstraction;
- BPM into reading time;
- stem into bank;
- EQ into grammar;
- crossfader into decorative slider.

Use:

- procedural score;
- form transformations;
- line/stanza/scene boundaries;
- relative weight;
- exact provenance.
